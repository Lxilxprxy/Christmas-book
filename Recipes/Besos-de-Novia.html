Besos de Novia

Description

Snowball cookies but known in Mexico as "Besos de Novia"
are sphere shaped cookies that taste of walnut, vanilla and almond.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. In a stand mixer, add the soft butter with the powdered sugar
    and mix on high speed until you get a pale-colored mix.
  2. Add the salt and vanilla to the mix and wait until it incorporates.
  3. Add the flour slowly to the mix on slow and finally add the walnuts until it's all mixed.
  4. Shape the cookie dough into balls and put them on a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake at 350 F or 180 C for 12 to 15 min.
  6. Roll the cooled cookies in powdered sugar and serve with milk.
