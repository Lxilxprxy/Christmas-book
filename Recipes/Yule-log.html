Yule Log

Description

Soft vanilla cake, rolled up to a delicious and light
whipped cream and strawberry jam and walnuts.
Decorated with powdered sugar and cookies.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Strawberry jam
  • Whipped cream
  • Powdered sugar
  • Cookies

Steps

  1. Separate the egg whites from the egg yolks. Mix 3/4 of the sugar with the egg whites to create a french meringue.
  2. Whip up the egg yolks with the rest of the sugar until you get to the ribbon stage. Mix up the oil, milk and vanilla extract in it.
  3. Add 1/3 of the whipped egg whites to the mix of egg yolk until fully incorporated. Then add it back to the egg whites, folding it without losing the bubbles.
  4. In a bowl, sift in the flour and baking powder, then add it to the other mixture in the same folding motion, very carefully until incorporated.
  5. On a large baking tray, lined with parchment paper and sprayed with vegetable oil, add the batter and bake at 325 F for 12 minutes.
  6. When the cake is ready, take out the oven and place on a tea towel with powdered sugar on it, then roll it and let it cool.
  7. Once cooled, put some of the whipped cream inside along with the strawberry jam, roll it back up and decorate with more whipping cream and cookies.
