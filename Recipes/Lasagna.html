Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna you have to serve your guests this Christmas.
With a layer of ricotta and spinach, as well as a homemade bolognese sauce, featuring red wine
this recipe is sure not to deceive anyone.

Ingredients

  • Store bought lasagna pasta
  • Homemade tomato sauce
  • Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Spinach
  • Ricotta
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 350 F.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, garlic, pepper, salt, parmesan, basil leaves and half the mozzarella.
  3. In a rectangular pyrex place some of the homemade tomato sauce and make layers of pasta, tomato sauce and the ricotta filling.
  4. Finish the lasagna with a layer of mozzarella and cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for at least 40min.
  6. To know if the pasta is cooked through, insert a knife through it and check if it's hard.
